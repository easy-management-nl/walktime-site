Privacy Policy

WalkTime

WalkTime respects your privacy.

Location Data

WalkTime uses your location only while the app is open and you actively use it to generate and follow walking routes. Location data is processed on your device and is never uploaded, stored on servers, or shared with third parties.

Saved Routes

Saved routes and walking history are stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted off your device.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. WalkTime does not receive or store any payment information.

Analytics

WalkTime does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Contact

If you have questions, contact: support@easy-management.nl
